HIGH POWER ROCKETRY

High Power Rocketry (HPR) is our program dedicated to teaching members how to design, build, and launch rockets capable of earning official Level 1 (L1) and Level 2 (L2) rocketry certifications. Members gain hands-on experience by constructing their own rockets from kits and preparing them for flight.

We partner with URRG (Upstate Research Rocketry Group) at their launch sites, giving our members access to safe, professional launch opportunities.
